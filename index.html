  • A solution for every shape

    iFLEXTUBE™ is made to provide you Design Freedom. iFLEXTUBE is a compact flexible linear LED lighting solution with topview and sideview series to conform to concave and convex surfaces with ease. With different cross sections, it provides the ability to incorporate more powerful LED chips which provide significantly higher lumen output. Light diffusion is done without compromise, eliminating any signs of spotting. With waterproof ratings up to IP68 it is impact, UV and saltwater resistant. The CLEARFILM versions feature 3M adhesive at the back and are cuttable on-site. With a range of anodised aluminium channels it can be installed at any place. The DIFFUSED versions are also available in custom lengths.

  • Countless Colors

    iFLEXTUBE™ enable creativity and augment your project identity. The RGBW versions are packed with powerful and high quality LEDs. It can accurately emit every color so that you can match the corporate identity or augment the architecture in the best possible way. Available in versions with viewing angle of up to 160 degrees it is ideal for exterior lighting applications for buildings so that your building stands out. The DYNAMIC WHITE versions offer tunable white light with temperatures ranging inbetween 2700K to 5000K. They are well suited to enhance the decor and provide good quality light.

  • Blooming beauty

    iFLEXTUBE™ allows you to take control of every pixel. PIXEL versions feature continuous lengths of 16 pixels per 2 meters and offer a resolution up to 125mm per pixel. Realising a low resolution graphic is designed with ease and is perfect for facade lighting applications. The dynamic effects can beautify your building and make your landmark stand out. iFLEXTUBE is compatible with DMX/RDM (0-100%), 0-10V (10-100%), or DALI (0- 100%) via iFlexpower Driver Series.

  • Professional Lighting

    iFLEXTUBE™ brings you quality of light for every application. High quality LED packages are selected for extended lifetime, efficiency and outstanding light quality. The CLEARFILM versions provide impressive color consistency with 3-step MacAdam ellipses. Available in 2400K, 2700K, 3000K, 4000K, 5000K and 6500K and a lumen output of up to 2000 per meter, with a minimum CRI of 80. Combined with a long lifespan of up to 50.000 hours it makes an excellent choice for hotels, resorts and high-end residential applications. iFLEXTUBE is designed for flexibility yet it is also engineered for reliability. Encapsulated in high performance silicone iFLEXTUBE can easily retain the aesthetic qualities and functionality of the silicone. It has no yellowing, no color-temperature shift and no efficiency drops.
